About KiraGizmos


KiraGizmos is a home-3D printing business that aims to provide fun gadgets, gizmos, toys, and tools that are homemade with care.

I currently use a Monoprice Voxel 3D printer, and source most of my prints from Thingiverse, Tinkercad, or they're edited/designed by me.

I'm based in south Florida, though I ship all over the country!

I use reused/recycled packaging and bubble wrap to decrease my environmental footprint and cut costs, which might make the package look a bit rough- but rest assured, the product is still packaged well inside!

I'm fully open to customized and custom prints! Message me on Etsy or email me (link in the top bar) if you'd like a custom print, or for any other questions.





How it all started


It started over the summer before 8th grade, when my dad got me a 3D printer and some classes on how to use it. It was my online instructor that introduced me to all the adorable 3D models online, which I started printing at home and giving out like candy to anyone that wanted one!

Soon, the comment, "Wow, you could totally sell these!" became increasingly common among my family and community, until my dad encouraged me to start an online shop and sell them! I did just that, and with both my parents' guidance on marketing, design, strategy, and business, I grew my little shop larger than I ever thought it would get.

Thanks to my parents, friends, community, and (most of all) Etsy shoppers for this awesome experience!
